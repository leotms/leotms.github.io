Hello World! I'm Leonardo Martínez - just in case you haven't noticed yet - and I'm a Computer Engineering Student at Universidad Simón Bolívar. I currently live in Caracas, Venezuela.

I love writing code. In the last year I've worked with AngularJS, Web2py, WordPress and Django for web development. I have also started working with Artificial Intelligence and Augmented Reallity in the past months.

As a former member of DELU, Universidad Simón Bolívar's video game development group, I've created a few video games. In fact, along with Aldrix Marfil, we've won Venezuela's Duel Jam 2 in 2016 with a small game called Fantasmotel, developed from scratch in 48 hours.

I think comunication and team work are fundamental skills for developers, and so I feel confident with agile development methodologies like SCRUM that promote this kind of work.

Reading and learning languages are among my hobbies. I also consider myself a fast learner. In the future I'd like to explore more about Android and Web Development.

In this site you'll find a list of my recent projects - not all of them, just my favourite ones - and my detailed CV.

If you want to keep in touch, please visit the contact section.

See you arround!
Leonardo.
